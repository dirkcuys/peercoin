What is peercoin?

Peer coin is a new currency for knowledge and skills where recognition, skills & knowledge doesn't come from a centralized authority, but rather gets distributed amoungs peers.

How does it work?

Peer coin gains value by user participating in peer coin communities and building connections with other peers.

Is it worth anything?

Many people are already doing useful productive work that is outside of the traditional idea of a carreer, using peer coin. And as the world is changing around us and work transforming, more people are moving towards peer coin.

How do I get some peer coin?

Anyone can generate peer coin at any time by participating in the community. This includes developing skills with peers, teaching peers on topics you know about, learning from peers about topics they know about and learning how to build strong peer connections.
